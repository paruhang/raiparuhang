Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF Merger safe to use for sensitive documents?

Yes, absolutely. This is a 100% client-side tool. Your files are processed directly in your browser and are never uploaded to any server. This makes it significantly more secure than traditional online PDF tools.

Is there a limit on file size or the number of PDFs?

There are no artificial limits. Since the merging happens on your device, the only limit is your computer's memory (RAM). You can merge as many files as you need for free.

Does this tool work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our tool is fully responsive and works on Chrome, Safari, and Firefox on both Android and iOS devices. No app installation is required.

Will my merged PDF have a watermark?

No. We believe in providing clean tools. Your final document will be watermark-free and exactly as you intended.
