Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF uploaded to a server?
Never. The conversion runs entirely in your browser using PDF.js (a Mozilla open-source library). Your file never leaves your device — no upload, no server, no data retained anywhere.
Which formats can I export to?
JPG (best for photos, smaller file size), PNG (lossless, supports transparency), and WebP (modern format with excellent compression). Choose the format that fits your use case.
Can I extract only specific pages?
Yes. Use the "Custom Range" option and enter page numbers or ranges like "1-3, 5, 8-10". You can also filter to odd or even pages only.
How do I get higher resolution output?
Increase the Render Scale in settings. 2× gives 144 dpi (great for screens), 3× gives 216 dpi, and 4× gives 288 dpi — suitable for print. Higher scales take more time and memory.
Can I download all pages at once?
Yes — click "Download All (ZIP)" to get every page in a ZIP archive. Or use "Select All" then "Download Selected" to grab just the pages you need.
